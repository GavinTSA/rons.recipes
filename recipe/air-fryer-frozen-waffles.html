Air Fryer Frozen Waffles

Ingredients

  • 4 frozen waffles

    • Equipment

  • Air Fryer
  • Spatula

    • Instructions

    1. Place frozen waffles in air fryer basket in one even layer, making sure not to overcrowd basket or stack waffles. Work in batches as needed depending on size of air fryer and size of waffles.
    2. Place basket in air fryer and set temperature to 350Â° Fahrenheit (if air fryer is not already preheated). Air fry waffles 3 minutes, then remove basket from air fryer.
    3. Carefully flip waffles over, then return basket to air fryer. Cook waffles another 1 to 2 minutes depending on desired doneness.
    4. Once waffles are cooked as desired, remove basket from air fryer and carefully transfer cooked waffles to plates. Add desired toppings and serve immediately.
