Air Fryer Italian Sausages

Ingredients

  • 5 Italian sausage links

    • Equipment

  • Air Fryer
  • Tongs
  • Internal meat thermometer

    • Instructions

    1. Preheat air fryer to 370Â° Fahrenheit.
    2. Once air fryer is preheated, remove basket from air fryer. Place 5 Italian sausage links in air fryer basket in one even layer, working in batches as needed to prevent overcrowding. Leave space around each sausage for air to circulate easily.
    3. Return basket to preheated air fryer. Air fry sausages 6 minutes.
    4. After 6 minutes, remove basket from air fryer and carefully flip sausages over. Return basket to air fryer and air fry sausages 4 minutes more.
    5. After 4 minutes, remove basket from air fryer and check internal temperature of sausages. Continue air frying sausages, checking doneness every 1 to 2 minutes, until internal temperature reaches 158Â° Fahrenheit.
    6. Once sausages reach desired temperature, carefully remove basket from air fryer and transfer sausages to large plate. Let sausages rest 3 to 5 minutes, then serve warm.
