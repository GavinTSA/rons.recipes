Instant Pot Bacon

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bacon

    • Equipment

  • Instant Pot

    • Instructions

    1. Place bacon in a single layer at the bottom of Instant Pot.
    2. Set to sauté for 5 minutes and cook, flipping frequently, until you’ve achieved your desired crispness.
