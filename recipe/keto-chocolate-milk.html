Keto Chocolate Milk (2)

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon hot water
  • 2-3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons Confectioners Swerve Sweetener
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • ½ cup heavy cream

    • Instructions

    1. Mix hot water and cocoa powder until combined.
    2. Add in powdered erythritol, milk, and cream. Stil until combined.
