Peanut Butter Maple Syrup

Ingredients

  • ½ cup maple syrup
  • ¼ cup creamy peanut butter

    • Instructions

    1. Mix ingredients in bowl.
    2. Heat in microwave at 30 second intervals. Whisk well to fully combine.
